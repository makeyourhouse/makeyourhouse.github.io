MYH Builders and Promoters Pvt Ltd.

MYH (Make Your House) provide a single platform to unorganised house building sectors.
Our mission is to eliminate labour chowk culture.Provide work to a manpower in the locality.

Benefits

Save Time: People can save their time. People will not have to roam outside and can easily book services from their phone sitting at home.
Save Money: People will save their money, they will not have to roam outside, to find out the needy manpower.
Save Energy: Sitting at home, you can call any services expert, so that their body remain good and their energy will also saved.

Purpose behind the MYH

Idea Behind this bussiness is to provide a single platform to the unorganised house building sector. The house building sector is unorganised till today. To find out work for themselves the manpower stands on labour chowk daily or put their contact no. at the street walls. In the same way, a house Builders is also roam around to find out needy manpower to construct their houses. Due to which the cost and time of construction incresed.
MYH "Make your House" will work like a bridge between a house builder and the manpower, like a labour, mason, POP designer, carpenter, marble finisher and tile fitter etc. MYH bring them on a single platform.
Through MYH a house dreamer can also reach to find out plot, architect, interior designer and ready to move in house.
